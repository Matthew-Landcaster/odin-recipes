Apple Pie

Description

Its an apple pie. Pie with apples in it. Apple pie.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Premade Pie Crusts
  • 6 Cups Thin Sliced, Peeled Apples
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp All Purpose Flour
  • 3/4 Tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt
  • 1/8 Tsp Ground Nutmeg
  • 1 Tbsp Lemon Juice

Steps

  • Heat oven to 425 °. Place 1 pie crust in an ungreased 9" glass pie plate. Press firmly against the sides and bottm.
  • In a large bowl mix together apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, salt, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Spoon mixture into the lined glass pie plate. top with the second crust. Wrap excess top crust under the bottom crust edge, pressing edges together to seal; flute. Cut slits into the top crust.
  • Bake 40-45 minutes or until apples are tender and crust is golden brown. Cover the edge of the crust with strips of foil after the first 15-20 minutes of baking to prevent excessive browning. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
