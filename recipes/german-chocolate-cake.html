German Choclate Cake

Description

A delicious chocolate cake that is incredibly easy to make and requires very few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 1 3/4 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 3/4 Cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1 Cup Buttermilk
  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Coconut Frosting

Steps

  • Heat oven to 375°, grease two 9" cake pans with cooking spray or vegetable oil, and begin to boil water.

  • Whisk together sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.

  • In a seperate bowl mix together eggs, buttermilk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.

  • Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until well combined. Once combined add boiled water to mixture and continue stiring until well incorperated.

  • Pour batter evenly into greased cake pans and bake 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out of the center with a few crumbs. Allow cakes to cool for a few minutes before transfering to a wire rack to finish cooling.

  • Once completely cooled frost and enjoy!
